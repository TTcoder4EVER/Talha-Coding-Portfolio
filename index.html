Project 1: GCSE to University converter.

This python program allows ambitious GCSE students to enter their grades, and see whether or not they would be able to go to the University of their choice, it works by looking at that university's entry requirments, whether or not it is in a foreign country in comparison to where the student is and then converting the student's GCSE grades to an approximate of their A-levels or S.A.T's, and then using that country's uni score conversion, like ATAR or UCAS to see if they are eligible.
